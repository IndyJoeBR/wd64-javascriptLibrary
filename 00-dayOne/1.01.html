First Day Notes:

Notes:

  • When using Emmet, class is noted by a "." and id is by "#"
  • HTML stands for HyperText Markup Language.
  • DOCTYPE states the typ eof our document. In this case, HTML.
  • Our HTML denotes our language to be in English per lang="en"
  • The "head" tag houses metadata ("meta"), or detailed data about our doc.
  • charset attribute specifies the character encoding (UTF-8); where each character has a numeric value.
  • name attribute "viewport" notes the device we are reading our document on
  • Title reflects the name and dsplays on the tab within our browswer.
  • A "style" tag can help us inject CSS into our document. NOT 'best practice'

Two Truths and a Lie

I've been to Santorini

I traveled to Santorini by hydrofoil from Mykonos.

I really enjoyed the grilled octopus on Therasia

Therasia is second of the main islands of Santorini. We had a day trip to it and the small volcanic cindercones in Santorini's caldera. There, we had lunch. I had the grilled octopus plate.

I stuck my hand in a volanic fumarole.

While walking on one of the two cindercones (Neakhamani), I noticed a hole in some rocks and just decided to stick my hand in it. Apparently the hot gasses have no color, but they are very hot.
